AI Agents in Make: Revolutionizing No-Code Automation

May 16, 2025

Make, a leading no-code automation platform, has introduced powerful AI agents that enable users to create intelligent workflows without any coding. These AI agents can automate tasks, generate content, analyze data, and make real-time decisions, dramatically expanding what's possible with automation.

With the latest updates, users can leverage top AI models (like GPT-4o, Gemini, Claude, and more) directly inside Make to:

  • Generate product descriptions, emails, or SEO keywords from images or prompts.
  • Analyze documents, images, or spreadsheets and process the data automatically.
  • Route tasks and automate decisions based on AI-driven analysis.
  • Connect seamlessly to tools like Google Sheets, Shopify, Etsy, Telegram, and any CRM.
