Beginner's Guide to E-commerce Automation

February 2025

What is E-commerce Automation? It's the use of technology to handle repetitive tasks in your online store, such as order processing, inventory updates, and customer notifications.

Why Automate? Automation saves time, reduces errors, and lets you focus on growing your business instead of manual work.

Where to Start:

  • Automate order tracking and notifications
  • Sync inventory across platforms
  • Use AI to generate product descriptions
  • Integrate with Google Sheets for bulk updates
